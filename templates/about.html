Welcome to Mango Hyper

Mango Hyper Central Market, established in 2003, is a leading multinational retail and wholesale entity based in Kuwait, with its corporate office situated in Shuwaikh. The organization has expanded rapidly, operating nine branches throughout Kuwait and employing over 400 individuals from diverse backgrounds, serving more than 1,000 customers daily.

Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Rafeeq Ahmad, Chairman and Managing Director, the group has successfully transitioned from traditional supermarket models to a high-profile retail and wholesale business. The hypermarkets are designed to provide a modern shopping experience, featuring spacious layouts, extensive product counters, ample parking, food courts, and fashion stores, all aimed at meeting the diverse needs of consumers under one roof.

Mango Hyper Central Market emphasizes quality in its products and services while actively pursuing new market opportunities to enhance value for its business partners. The company's operations are guided by core values of Integrity, Teamwork, Accountability, Innovation, and Commitment, which underpin its mission to improve the overall shopping experience for customers.
